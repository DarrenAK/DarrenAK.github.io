My Works

Conscious Coffee is a concept coffee site fictionally operated throughout Oslo, Norway. The purpose of the site was to provide information to the on-the-go user. Information included opening and closing hours as well as locations around town. The emphasis on mobile responsiveness was a priority for this site.

Built with bootstrap.

Snug is a concept e-commerce site focused on usability. The company sells child carriers. The intended audience for this site is withing the age range of 20-70. Meaning anyone with a family or working in child care. Everything provided within the site can be found within three "clicks" and navigation is intuitive. Simple and clean design.

Built with bootstrap.
